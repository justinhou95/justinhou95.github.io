## Introduction

This is a course study report in Financial Engineering (Fall 2019) on the paper "Deep Hedging" by Hans Bühler, Lukas Gonon, Josef Teichmann, and Ben Woodfrom. We are very interested in how deep neural network can be applied in hedging problems. Therefore, we choose this paper for our course study project. Their approach expand our views on solving hedging problems beyond solving high dimensional PDEs, so we also invite readers of curiosities to take a look at it.
