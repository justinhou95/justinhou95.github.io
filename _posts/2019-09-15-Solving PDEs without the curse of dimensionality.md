## Introduction

In this paper, we strengthen the bound of the generalization error of empirical risk minimization (ERM) over deep neural network hypothesis classes without the constraint of the width of neural network and no further constraint is introduced in our bound. Beyond this main result in our paper, we present an explicit expression of the optimal solution of mathematical learning problem with quadratic loss in a general setting. Moreover, some detail argument are added in the proof of the covering number theorem for the deep neural network space.
