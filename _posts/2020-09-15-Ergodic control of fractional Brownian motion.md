## Introduction

In this paper, we introduce a new machine learning algorithm to solve the HJB equation of the corresponding ergodic control problem of the approximating OU processes. This method is invented based on the backward stochastic differential equation formulation of ergodic control problem and shows great potential solving
ergodic control problem of fBM when no analytical solution is known or when the dimension of approximating process is large.
